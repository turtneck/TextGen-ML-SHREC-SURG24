Irelands historic vote in favor of marriage equality last Friday was in the end not especially close with the yesses carrying almost every electoral district Still as Jason Kellehers map of the vote shows there were some very pronounced regional differencesAnyone familiar with the basic sociology of gay rights in the West will be unsurprised to see that support for equality is largest in Dublin and down south in Cork Irelands second city Support was generally weakest in the northern and western parts of the country roughly the area known as ConnaughtBut overall the picture is of enormously broad support for the referendum including in most of rural Ireland
