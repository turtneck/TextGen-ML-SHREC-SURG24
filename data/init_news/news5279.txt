speeches by Texas Republican Sen Ted Cruz and New Jerseys Democratic Sen Bob Menendez arguing respectively for and against the Sanctuary Cities Bill the Senate voted 5446 against the legislationThe bill has divided Congress along partisan lines and gained national attention in the wake of GOP presidential candidate Donald Trumps harsh words on immigration and the shooting death of a California
