We do not like trampolining say hedgehogs 101116 HEDGEHOGS have confirmed they do not like trampolining and children should not make them do it Britains hedgehogs attacked this years John Lewis Christmas advert featuring trampolining woodland animals warning that children would now try to kidnap hedgehogs and force them to bounce up and down Hedgehog Roy Hobbs said I accidentally wandered across a trampoline once I hated it and I wasnt even bouncing up and down It was just all wobbly underfoot Made me queasy The hedgehog in this advert is not real It is an unnatural computer generated abomination Hobbs added Hedgehogs are very much groundbased creatures We eat grubs and roll into a ball for protection In our leisure time we watch stuff like Bake Off and Orange is the New Black  The most excitement we have is the occasional game of Buckaroo Anyway keep your brats on a leash or well give them scabies Share
