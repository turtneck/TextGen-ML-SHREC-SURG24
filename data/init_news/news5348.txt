military operation including streetbystreet fighting backed by heavy airstrikes and artillery fire to eradicate Daesh militants in Sirte They have so far recaptured most areas in the city Most of Daesh commanders in the city fled to other places and the remaining rank has been holding out via sniper fire booby traps and car bombs Sirte is the only key base of the Takfiri militant group outside
