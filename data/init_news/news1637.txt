formulating the UNs official position on conflicts occurring around the world as well as the domestic policies of member states The elections took place against a backdrop of criticism from nongovernmental human rights organizations who say that the body has been hijacked by oppressive regimes looking to deflect criticism and drive their own agendas Human Rights Watch and Amnesty
